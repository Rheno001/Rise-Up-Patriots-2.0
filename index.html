About Rise Up Patriots Conference 2.0

The "Rise Up, Patriots Conference 2.0" of the Unveiling and Rebranding Nigeria Initiative (URNI) is themed "Projecting the Positive Image of Nigeria through the Creative Ecosystems".

This annual forum brings together policymakers, thought leaders, creatives (entertainers, content creators, journalists, photographers and experts in AI) and over 1000 young Nigerians for robust conversations on patriotism, governance, and national development.

This significant event, themed "Rise up Patriot Conference 2.0" marks a pivotal moment in our collective journey to inspire patriotism and foster national pride among Nigerians.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the URNI Rise Up Patriot Conference 2.0 below.

The URNI Rise Up Patriots Conference 2.0 is a comprehensive gathering focused on patriotic values, community building, and national discourse. This conference brings together thought leaders, activists, and citizens to discuss important issues facing our nation and explore solutions for positive change.

The conference is open to all citizens who are passionate about patriotic values and community engagement. We welcome individuals from all backgrounds, professions, and walks of life who are interested in constructive dialogue and positive action for our country.

Registration can be completed online through our official website. Simply visit the registration page, fill out the required information.
